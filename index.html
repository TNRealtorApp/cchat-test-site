How It Works

  1. Pick your decade. Choose one of our four featured eras or request a custom departure (subject to causal review and a small paradox surcharge).
  2. Suit up at our Knoxville hub. Costume fitting, paperwork, and a brisk temporal-etiquette briefing. Please leave smartphones, opinions, and crypto behind.
  3. Step through. Three minutes of polite vertigo, then you're there. Pickup is automatic in seventy-two subjective hours, give or take a war.
